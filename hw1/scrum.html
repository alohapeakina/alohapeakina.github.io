Scrum Overview


Scrum is a presriptive agile framework which advocates for incremental and iterative software development. Most famous for the "sprint" terminology, the principles of scrum advocate for self-organized teams which operate in short iteration cycles. Providing timeboxed work cycles enables teams to rapidly respond to feedback through reviewing progress and comparing it to the needs of the software being developed.
